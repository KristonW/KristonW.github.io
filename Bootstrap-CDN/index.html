Design Thesis

My design Thesis below shows how I came up with the logo for Kiwi and how I even named my company Kiwi

The reason I chose these colors for my palette is a result of my favorite color combinations. I chose Uranian Blue and Coral Pink because they just give me a vibrant feeling. I chose Yale Blue and Caramel to contrast the pastel colors. I chose name Kiwi because of the fruit and the correlation between my first initial of my first name and first initial of my middle name. For the overall design of the logo I took Inspiration from the windows 10 logo and how the rectangles felt 3d image like and I took my own spin onto that.
